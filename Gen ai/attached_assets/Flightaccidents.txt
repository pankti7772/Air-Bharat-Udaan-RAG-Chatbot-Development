AIR INDIA – ACCIDENTS / INCIDENTS

1. Date: 27 December 1947
Type: Accident
Aircraft: Douglas C-48C (DC-3)
Location: Near Karachi, Pakistan
Fatalities: 23
Description: First documented crash of an Air India aircraft. The plane crashed shortly after takeoff due to poor visibility and possible instrument failure.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Date: 3 November 1950
Type: Accident
Flight: Air India Flight 245 (Malabar Princess)
Aircraft: Lockheed L-749 Constellation
Location: Mont Blanc, France
Fatalities: 48
Description: The aircraft crashed into Mont Blanc while descending toward Geneva during poor weather conditions.

--------------------------------------------------

3. Date: 11 April 1955
Type: Accident / Bombing
Flight: Air India Flight 300 (Kashmir Princess)
Aircraft: Lockheed L-749A Constellation
Location: South China Sea near Indonesia
Fatalities: 16
Description: A bomb exploded on board mid-flight causing the aircraft to crash into the sea.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Date: 24 January 1966
Type: Accident
Flight: Air India Flight 101 (Kanchenjunga)
Aircraft: Boeing 707
Location: Mont Blanc, France
Fatalities: 117
Description: The aircraft crashed into Mont Blanc during descent to Geneva due to navigation error.

--------------------------------------------------

5. Date: 1 January 1978
Type: Accident
Flight: Air India Flight 855
Aircraft: Boeing 747
Location: Arabian Sea near Mumbai
Fatalities: 213
Description: Instrument malfunction caused spatial disorientation of the pilot, leading the aircraft to crash into the sea.

--------------------------------------------------

6. Date: 26 November 1981
Type: Hijacking
Flight: Air India Flight 224
Aircraft: Boeing 707
Location: Seychelles
Fatalities: None
Description: Armed militants hijacked the aircraft during refueling. After negotiations, the hijackers surrendered and passengers were released.

--------------------------------------------------

7. Date: 21 June 1982
Type: Accident
Flight: Air India Flight 403
Aircraft: Boeing 707
Location: Mumbai, India
Fatalities: 17
Description: Aircraft skidded off the runway during heavy rain and poor visibility.

--------------------------------------------------

8. Date: 23 June 1985
Type: Terrorist Bombing
Flight: Air India Flight 182 (Emperor Kanishka)
Aircraft: Boeing 747
Location: Atlantic Ocean near Ireland
Fatalities: 329
Description: A bomb hidden in cargo exploded mid-flight. This remains the deadliest disaster in Air India history.

--------------------------------------------------

9. Date: 22 May 2010
Type: Accident
Flight: Air India Express Flight 812
Aircraft: Boeing 737-800
Location: Mangalore, India
Fatalities: 158
Description: Aircraft overshot the runway during landing at the hilltop airport and plunged into a gorge.

--------------------------------------------------

10. Date: 7 August 2020
Type: Accident
Flight: Air India Express Flight 1344
Aircraft: Boeing 737-800
Location: Kozhikode, India
Fatalities: 21
Description: The aircraft overshot the runway in heavy monsoon rain and split into two sections.

--------------------------------------------------

11. Date: 12 June 2025
Type: Accident
Flight: Air India Flight 171
Aircraft: Boeing 787-8 Dreamliner
Location: Ahmedabad, India
Fatalities: 241 (plus casualties on ground)
Description: The aircraft crashed shortly after takeoff due to fuel supply switches turning off, causing engine failure.

INDIGO AIRLINES – INCIDENTS

1. Date: 14 December 2016
Type: Incident
Flight: IndiGo 6E201
Aircraft: Airbus A320-232
Location: Raipur, India
Description: The aircraft experienced a hard landing while arriving from Delhi. Minor damage occurred but passengers were safe.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Date: 23 August 2022
Type: Incident
Aircraft: Airbus A320 Neo (VT-IZR)
Location: Goa, India
Description: A technical issue occurred during flight operations. The aircraft landed safely and investigation followed.

--------------------------------------------------

3. Date: 13 September 2022
Type: Incident
Aircraft: Airbus A320 (VT-IFN)
Location: Ahmedabad, India
Description: Operational incident during landing procedures. No injuries reported.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Date: 30 December 2022
Type: Incident
Aircraft: ATR 72-600 (VT-IRA)
Location: Jodhpur, India
Description: Technical issue reported and investigated by aviation authorities.

--------------------------------------------------

5. Date: 2 January 2023
Type: Incident
Aircraft: Airbus A321 (VT-ILR)
Location: Kolkata Airport
Description: Tail-strike occurred while landing. Aircraft landed safely.

--------------------------------------------------

6. Date: 11 February 2023
Type: Incident
Aircraft: Airbus A320 (VT-ITP)
Route: Bangalore to Rajkot
Description: Operational issue during flight. Aircraft landed safely.

--------------------------------------------------

7. Date: 10 June 2023
Type: Incident
Aircraft: Airbus A321 (VT-ILN)
Location: Delhi Airport
Description: Aircraft returned to airport shortly after takeoff due to a technical issue.

--------------------------------------------------

8. Date: October 2025
Type: Incident
Description: Passenger power bank caught fire on Delhi–Dimapur flight. Crew controlled the fire and passengers remained safe.

--------------------------------------------------

9. Date: 2025
Type: Incident
Description: ATR aircraft experienced temporary engine flameout during heavy rain on the Jaipur–Chandigarh route but landed safely.
